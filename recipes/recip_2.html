Chopped Chimichurri Steak Salad

This salad is a vibrant and flavorful dish that combines tender grilled steak with fresh vegetables and a zesty chimichurri dressing. It's perfect for a light lunch or dinner!

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb flank steak
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Mixed salad greens (arugula, spinach, romaine)
  • Cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Cucumber, sliced
  • Red onion, thinly sliced

Instructions:

  1. Season the flank steak with salt and pepper.
  2. Grill the steak to your desired doneness, then let it rest before slicing.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, garlic, parsley, cilantro, and red pepper flakes to make the chimichurri dressing.
  4. In a large bowl, combine the salad greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
  5. Toss the salad with the chimichurri dressing.
  6. Top with the sliced grilled steak and serve immediately.
